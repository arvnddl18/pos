# GCash personal flow (runbook)

1. Cashier selects **GCash** tender and enters amount; POS shows **pending**.
2. Customer sends to your **personal GCash** number or scans your **Receive QR** (outside the POS).
3. Staff verifies on the **shop GCash app** (notification) or customer proof screen.
4. Staff taps **Confirm GCash received** on the pending payment.
5. For disputes (amount mismatch, no notification): manager opens GCash history, reconciles with ticket. Void line or refund per policy.

Large amounts: set **Manager confirm above** in GCash settings so only managers complete confirmation.
